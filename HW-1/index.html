My name is, Jacob Williams, and I’m a media arts student living in Kalispell, MT. My current dream is to one day become a part of the animation industry for either cartoons or video games, as it has been my greatest passion since childhood.

I am currently an undergraduate student in my second year, taking online classes for the School of Visual and Performing Arts at the University of Montana.  After I complete all of my credits this year, I am hoping to graduate with my Bachelor of Arts degree in Media Arts, and looking forward to entering the job market in search of a position involving digital and graphic arts.

For as long as I can remember, I’ve been one of those cartoon and video game fanatics. Since then, I became interested in drawing and designing characters and animated adventures of my very own.  I brought these passions along with me throughout high school and college, and learned about programs like; the Adobe Creative Suites, Maya Autodesk, and website design.  Before U of M, I had previously attended Flathead Valley Community College, where I earned two degrees; an AAS in Graphic Design, and an AA in General Studies.

I have had plenty of past experiences in web design, and believe me, it is not easy. It is a very detail oriented program, but when you get the hang of it, it is fun, especially when you get to view all the hard work you’ve done at the end.
